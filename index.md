# About me

I am a philosophy Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California, where I am advised primarily by Andrew Bacon. Before USC, I've done graduate work at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (University of Amsterdam) and at the University of St Andrews. My undergraduate degree is from the University of Padua. 

I work mostly on topics in philosophical logic and metaphysics. I also have interests in the philosophy of language, decision theory, formal semantics,  and mathematical logic.
